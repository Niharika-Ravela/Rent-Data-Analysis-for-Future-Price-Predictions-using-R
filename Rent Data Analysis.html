Rent Data Analysis for Future Price Prediction

Dataset used

data_transactions.csv

Target Variable and audience

Target Variable

The target variable in the dataset is the nominal transaction price (column name: pricetransaction_nominal). This is the transaction price in dollars ($), nominal.

Audience

This project is aimed at apartment owners and other related parties who are interested at knowing the nominal transaction price of an apartment.

Lead Statement

Apartment owners, especially new ones, are facing a lot of difficulties in planning for their aparment transactions due to insufficient or non-uniform data available which makes them end up making un-informed decisions.
